About Me

Hey there! I'm Shane — a developer who believes the best code is written after a great cup of coffee. I spend my days exploring the intersection of artificial intelligence and creative problem-solving, and my evenings tinkering with open-source projects.

Whether I'm training neural networks, perfecting my pour-over technique, or diving into a new programming language, I'm always chasing that perfect blend of curiosity and craft. This blog is where I share what I learn along the way.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me gaming, making music, or chasing the perfect photo. I believe life is richer when you stay curious — and well-caffeinated.
